Victorial Falls Vikings Club

The Victoria Falls Vikings Rugby Club, founded in April 2024, aims to grow rugby in the Victoria Falls region. Led by Captain Sean Sami, the club focuses on developing players of all ages through training and competition. They are actively involved in the community and share updates on their Instagram and other social media platforms.
